reGen - brand creation

reGen is brand created by me for academic purposes only. This is a company of technological solutions related to medicine. It is in charge of its manufacture, distribution and implementation. As part of the objectives of this academic project i also created a campaign through two posters that you can see below. The video you can see on the right was also developed as part of this project as a little intro that can be used in TV spots or even in social networks.

the symbol

This brand symbol was designed based on the forms of representation of the Vitruvian Man from Leonardo Da Vinci's 1490 drawing. The final presentation was, however, rethought and modified to have a graphic presentation. In the end was complemented with the lettering "reGen", the name of the brand, to finish the entire logo.
